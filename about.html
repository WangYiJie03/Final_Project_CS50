Jay Chou's story started when he wrote 50 songs in 10days. His boss realised 10 songs that he wrote and he become famous.

Lifestory

After more than half a year of careful production, Jay Chou's first album "Jay" was produced. In early 2001, "Jay" sold very well. In the selection process of the Taiwan Golden Melody Awards that year, "Jay" won the Best Pop Vocal Album Award in one fell swoop. After that, Jay Chou became famous in one fell swoop, releasing albums, directing and acting in movies and TV series. Producer, magician, host, etc., more and more people love him (but it is also because of his talent). became the current king
